About EcoCycle

EcoCycle is a circular economy platform dedicated to reducing waste and promoting sustainable consumption. By connecting users with repair services, upcyclers, and a marketplace for refurbished goods, we aim to:

  • Reduce landfill waste by 40% through material recovery
  • Extend product lifecycles by 300% on average
  • Create economic value from discarded materials
  • Lower carbon emissions through localized circular systems

Every transaction on our platform prevents an average of 15kg of CO2 emissions and keeps 5kg of materials in circulation.
